About Guru Raj Kumar


Guru Raj Kumar

Guru Raj Kumar has been deeply influenced by spirituality and mantra sadhana since childhood. With the support of his family, guidance from enlightened gurus, unknown seekers, and sacred spiritual books, he immersed himself in the study and practice of ancient mantras and their profound meanings.

In 2005, he founded Akshar Mantra Tantra Yantra with a vision to share the wisdom of mantra sadhana, spiritual consultancy, and mantra courses with people seeking peace and purpose. Since then, he has guided countless individuals toward inner transformation and higher consciousness through authentic spiritual practices.

With years of deep practice and experience, Guru Raj Kumar developed a unique and simplified method to make mantra knowledge practical and accessible to everyone. His teachings focus on using mantra energy for peace, success, and prosperity in everyday life.

Along with his spiritual journey, Guru Raj Kumar also follows a disciplined approach to financial growth through trading. By applying his self-created Shopkeeper Strategy, he balances both spiritual and financial growth, living a peaceful and content life while spreading divine knowledge.

Today, Guru Raj Kumar continues to expand the reach of mantra wisdom through spiritual consultancy, mantra courses, and guided practices. His mission is to help every individual recognize their inner power and manifest their true potential through the transformative power of mantras.
