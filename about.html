About Akshar Mantra Tantra Yantra


Gurudev Raj Kumar

Akshar Mantra Tantra Yantra, established on December 1, 2005 by Shri Akshar Ji, is a manifestation and healing center dedicated to personal and spiritual growth. We provide authentic teachings on mantra science, meditation, self-hypnosis, and spiritual healing to help individuals connect deeply with their inner energy and transform their lives.

Our Vision

To bring ancient Indian spiritual wisdom into modern daily life — enabling people to manifest desires, achieve mental peace, and live with clarity and confidence. For over two decades, Akshar Mantra Tantra Yantra has been guiding seekers toward inner awakening and abundance.

What We Offer

  • Mantra and meditation courses for transformation
  • Spiritual healing sessions using sacred sound and energy balance
  • Self-hypnosis training for manifestation and clarity
  • Guided practices for emotional healing and calmness

Our Philosophy

True healing begins within. When your thoughts, emotions, and vibrations align with divine energy, life automatically attracts harmony and success. We combine ancient mantra knowledge with modern mind-science to help seekers live balanced, peaceful, and spiritually empowered lives.

Founder

Shri Akshar Ji — spiritual guide, healer, and founder of Akshar Mantra Tantra Yantra — has devoted his life to spreading the transformative power of mantra and meditation. His vision is to awaken divine awareness in people and help them experience healing through consciousness and sound.
